Geek Kumbakonam

Geek IIC Kumbakonam is an innovation incubation cell in kumbakonam. Our motive is to intend the inspirational enthu in the hearts of the Students. This IIC is to give opportunity to the people and to enhance their skills in the tech area. The GEEK IIC Kumbakonam is an innovation incubation cell in MASS College of Arts and Science, which is organized by Geek Community to share knowledge in the Technology area. Geek IIC actively grabs members in Kumbakonam, students engaged in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology and giving Training for the members.
